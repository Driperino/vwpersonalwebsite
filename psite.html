What even is a personal website?

This project will be updated over the course of my program demonstrating all the things I learn! A well-crafted portfolio website serves as a dynamic showcase of your skills, experiences, and accomplishments, offering potential employers an insightful glimpse into your professional journey. By curating a portfolio that highlights your projects, achievements, and expertise, you not only demonstrate your capabilities but also establish your credibility and professionalism in your field. A portfolio website provides a centralized platform for recruiters and hiring managers to assess your suitability for positions, allowing them to explore your work samples, testimonials, and credentials at their convenience. Additionally, a carefully designed website can help you stand out in a competitive job market, leaving a lasting impression on recruiters and increasing your chances of securing interviews and job offers. In essence, a portfolio website serves as a powerful tool for personal branding, showcasing your unique value proposition and positioning you as a top candidate in your industry.
